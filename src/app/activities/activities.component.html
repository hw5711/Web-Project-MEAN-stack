10. Sports activities and Parties A student can use the website to find out the sports activities and parties scheduled. The student can enter a time period, for example, September 1 2012 to December 31 2012. The website can list all the scheduled activities during this period. The student can select the activities that he/she wants to attend. And all the activities chosen by the student should be displayed by month.
